About the App

Think you know football? Prove it with Football Pronostic, the ultimate football prediction game! Guess match results, compete with friends, and see who’s the real football prediction master.

With an easy and fun design, you can make your predictions in seconds and track your progress after every match.

Main Features

  • Make Predictions: Predict win, draw, or loss for each match.
  • Play with Friends: Join groups and see who gets the most points.
  • Live Stats: Check how many users think your team will win.
  • Match Reminders: Never miss a game or a chance to predict.
  • All Major Leagues: From Champions League to Premier League, LaLiga, World Cup, Serie A, and more.

Why You’ll Love It

Football Pronostic is made for real football fans. Whether you play for fun or competition, it’s the best way to enjoy football even more. Challenge your friends, share your results, and climb to the top of the leaderboard!
